"One Small Step For Man,

One Giant Leap For Mankind"

The Apollo series of missions were conducted with the aim of
Beating the Soviets in landing the first man on the moon
Exploration of the moon for scientific gain and
Developing technology to secure other national interests.

Apollo Missions Timeline

Apollo 1

The first mission of the Apollo series, the maiden flight of the Apollo program ended in tragedy as flames claimed the control panel and three astronauts lost their lives. No missions were ever named Apollo 2 or 3.
