LIFE AT NASA

There are jobs and there are careers. But at NASA, our work is more than just a profession — it’s a lifelong pursuit, a passion — and a chance to change the history of humanity. For more than 50 years, NASA has been breaking barriers to achieve the seemingly impossible — from walking on the Moon to pushing the boundaries of human spaceflight farther than ever before. Our more than 18,000 professionals come from many diverse backgrounds, but are united by a common purpose: to pioneer the future in space exploration, scientific discovery and aeronautics research. In addition to our core values, our NASA team embodies a few shared characteristics that are integral to our success.
