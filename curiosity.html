Curiosity, The Mars Rover
"From the Red Planet's Frontier."

Habitat: Mars?

Curiosity is a part of the Mars Exploration Program in which,
we here at NASA are attempting to uncover more about the red
planet. Curiosity's role is to find if life could've ever been
supported in Mars's environment, to see if Mars was habitable
at any point in the planet's long history.

Curiosity helps us to survey Mars's habitability via the
equipment it has on board to perform analysis on martian soil
and minerals. It is able to see the chemical makeup of these
